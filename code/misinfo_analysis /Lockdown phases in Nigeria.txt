March 29, 2020 - Buhari announces the first lockdown in the Major cities; Lagos, Abuja, and Ogun
Link to speech: https://nairametrics.com/2020/03/29/president-muhammadu-buharis-full-speech-on-covid-19-pandemic/


March 30, 2020 - Lockdown Begins in Lagos, Ogun, and Abuja. Was supposed to be for 2 weeks


April 13, 2020 - Buhari’s second speech extending the lockdown for another two weeks
        Link to speech: https://nairametrics.com/2020/04/13/president-muhammadu-buharis-full-speech-on-extended-covid-19-lockdown/


April 27, 2020 - Buhari’s third speech announcing a “Phased and gradual” easing of the lockdown and imposing a curfew from 8pm to 6am
        Link to speech:
https://nairametrics.com/2020/04/27/fill-speech-of-president-buhari-on-covid-19-pandemic/


May 2, 2020 - First phase of Lockdown ease


June 2, 2020 - Second phase of lockdown ease. Curfew from 10pm to 4am


June 30, 2020 - Third phase of lockdown ease allowing the reopening of airports for domestic flights and school resumption for graduating students.
